find the way home

As society develops, pets have become an indispensable part of many families. Domesticated pets have accompanied humans through the hunting era, the agricultural era, and into today's modern age. However, in recent years, incidents of pet abandonment and abuse have been on the rise. Statistics show that in the United States, approximately 3.9 million dogs are abandoned each year, and around 70 million cats are homeless. Every
60 seconds, an animal suffers abuse at the hands of humans, with numbers
increasing annually. These alarming phenomena not only pose risks to societal development but also impact people's physical and mental well-being. Therefore, this project aims to address the issues of pet abandonment and abuse through a reflective and healing game. The goal is for players to experience the impact of abandonment and abuse on pets within the game. Additionally, it is hoped that the game's healing colors and cute animal companions will increase people's love and attention towards animals, helping to reduce incidents of
animal harm.

Designed a game that combines reflection and entertainment, aiming to raise awareness and inspire change through an innovative approach.
In the game, players experience the world from the first-person perspective of anabandoned puppy. Throughout various scenarios, players help mistreated kittens,navigate survival challenges faced after abandonment, and complete a series of tasks. By interacting with other animal residents and collecting clues, players gradually uncover the truth, form bonds with different animal friends, and ultimately seek their way back home.
The game places a strong emphasis on healing and empathy. With vibrant visuals and enchanting animal interactions,it aims to spark players’ interest in and affection for the animal world, raising awareness about vulnerable groups,particularly abandoned animals. The game features two modes: an adventure mode and a sandbox creation mode.In adventure mode, players can engage with emotionally charged narratives and reflect on societal issues, while thesandbox mode offers a space for creativity and the freedom to build their own healing environment.
Through this game, I hope players not only gain a deeper understanding of the problemssurrounding pet abandonment and abuse but also recognize the importance of fosteringharmonious relationships between humans and animals, as well as among people themselves. Mayevery player find resonance in this journey and pass on the warmth they feel to the real world.
