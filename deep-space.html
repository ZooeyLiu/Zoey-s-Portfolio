DEEP SPACE

SCI-FI SHORT FILM CREATED BY UNREAL ENGINE

"Deep Space" is a sci-fi short film that follows the journey of Kelly, a scientist determined to uncover the truth behind her friend Jack's mysterious death on Ceres, a distant asteroid. Set in the year 2077, the film explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and ethical conflicts in the age of advanced space exploration.

Set in the year 2077, the story unfolds five years after the "Deep Space Operation" mineral exploration mission on Ceres has gone silent. The protagonist, "Kelly," is on a quest to find her old friend, "Jack," who went missing after joining the mission five years ago. However, Kerry's other friend, "Ash," peculiarly tries to prevent her from embarking on this adventure. It seems that the incident from five years ago may not be as simple as it appears...
