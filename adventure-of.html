In the ancient Chinese medical book Treatise on Febrile Diseases, the littleblue dragon recipe of the small green dragon formula accidentally travels to thereal world and becomes a clumsy paper character. In order to find his identity,he leads medical students who are reading Treatise on Febrile Diseases into thewonderful world of books. From climbing mountains and collecting herbs, tostudying the ancient method of cooking the small green dragon soup, he guidesthem to explore the profound inner meaning of traditional Chinese medicineculture as shown in the medical book Treatise on Febrile Diseases.In this project, our team explored how animation and film can be effectivelycombined to create an engaging and educational piece with cultural significance.Through the interactions between the Little Blue Dragon character and theprotagonist, the film introduces audiences to the rich traditions of Chinesemedicine in an entertaining way. This project highlights the impact of interactiveand immersive media in cultural promotion, showcasing how these techniquescan deepen understanding and appreciation for traditional practices like those inChinese medicine.
