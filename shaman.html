TD Effect Experiment

Water droplet collision

The main element used in this effect is the shape of water droplets. Water droplets flow, collide and splash to form a dynamic pattern of ripples, reflecting the water element corresponding to Mercury in shaman elements.

Main Tone: Blue / Purple / White

Water Wave Flow

The main characteristic of this effect is the flow of particles, which shows more of the flow dynamics of water compared to water droplets hitting. Water particles gather and disperse, which also corresponds to the water element of Mercury.

Main Tone: Blue / Purple / Blue-Green / White

Concept (Optional)

This project explores the intersection of ancient shamanic rituals and futuristic alien civilizations. By systematizing the Wu Xing (Five Elements) into a digital matrix, we create a scalable framework for character design. Each 'Shaman' acts as a medium, translating their planetary element into visual reality through real-time TouchDesigner projections, bridging the gap between physical craft and digital performance.
