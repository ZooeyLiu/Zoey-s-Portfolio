INTO THE UNKNOWN

First Person
Mystery Game
In this game, players take on the role of acourageous journalist infiltrating an illegal "blackfactory" to gather evidence and retrieve a hiddenemployee list from a secure safe, with the goalof freeing exploited workers. As players progress,they’ll need to solve puzzles to unlock new areasand avoid detection by security. The mission is toobtain key evidence and make it out safely.This game combines elements of puzzle-solvingand stealth action, offering players a thrillingexperience of investigation, risk, and discovery.Beyond entertainment, it aims to raise awarenessabout real-world issues of exploitation and socialjustice.

Core Mechanics

In certain levels of the game, players must evade patrolling security guards, solving puzzles and gathering evidence without being spotted or attacked.
